Rainier Beach is a neighborhood in the city of Seattle, WA that is experiencing significant levels of gentrification and displacement following a tech boom and subsequent housing shortage.

This housing project addresses the need for an increased supply of housing units that serve the specific needs of the Rainier Beach community. Important demographics that were considered in the planning and design are as follows:

3 people/household average (with 2/household in Seattle)
30.1% of households with children (19.6% in Seattle)
60.1% housing burdened (34.6% in Seattle)
72.7% BIPOC (34.3% in Seattle)
32.5% non-US born (19.4% in Seattle)
